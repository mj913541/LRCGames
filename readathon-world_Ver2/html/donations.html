Families can support our Read-A-Thon in three easy ways — and you can use any combination of them. Every donation helps grow our Learning Resource Center with fresh, high-interest books and exciting student rewards.

Where does the money go?

20% of proceeds go back to students to spend in the Prize Store. The remaining 80% goes directly to the LRC for books.
