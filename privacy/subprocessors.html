LRC Quest — Subprocessor List

Last updated: January 2026

LRC Quest occasionally uses carefully selected third-party service providers (“subprocessors”) to support secure delivery of our services. These providers process Student Data only under the direction of the Local Education Agency (LEA) and only for educational purposes.

Important: LRC Quest does not sell student data, does not use student data for targeted advertising, and does not share student data with third parties except as described below and as required to operate the service.
